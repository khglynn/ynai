## "Stamp to Approve" Concept Expansion

The genius of "stamp" is the **physical action metaphor** - it feels like you're DOING something satisfying. Other actions with that energy:

### Physical Approval Actions
- **Stamp** - official seal of approval
- **Punch** - time clock, ticket punch
- **Click** - satisfying button press
- **Slide** - swipe to approve
- **Drop** - drop into the right bucket
- **File** - satisfying filing cabinet thunk
- **Seal** - wax seal, envelope closure
- **Mark** - checking off a list
- **Tag** - labeling, categorizing
- **Slot** - coin into slot machine

### Vintage Office Sounds
- **Ker-chunk** - stamp press
- **Ka-ching** - cash register
- **Click-clack** - typewriter
- **Ding** - service bell
- **Thunk** - file drawer closing

## Notes

- Short names (4-6 chars) are better for typing
- `.app` and `.io` are tech-friendly
- `.ai` is perfect for AI-powered tools
- Avoid names that are hard to spell or pronounce
- The cartoon/vintage vibe should come through in the name OR the branding, not necessarily both
