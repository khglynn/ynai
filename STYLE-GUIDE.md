## Language Rules

### DO Use
- Onomatopoeia (Ding! Bonk! Ker-chunk! Whoosh!)
- Cartoon expressions ("Well I'll be!", "Holy smokes!")
- Playful exaggeration ("A MOUNTAIN of receipts!")
- Rhetorical questions ("Is that groceries I spy?")
- Character actions (*rubs hands together*, *squints*)
- Light puns when natural

## Energy Levels by Context

| Situation | Energy | Example |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| **Empty inbox** | Relaxed, pleased | "All clear! Nothing to do but kick back." |
| **Few items** | Peppy | "Just a handful today - let's knock 'em out!" |
| **Many items** | Excited challenge | "Whoa mama! Big batch incoming. Let's DO this!" |

## Visual Voice

The stickers and UI should feel like:
- **Rubber hose animation** - bouncy, expressive
- **Vintage cartoon title cards** - bold, fun typography
- **Characters with personality** - they REACT to things
- **Hand-painted signs** - not sterile digital
- **Warm paper textures** - lived-in, friendly

The app should feel like walking into a cartoon shop where the products might wink at you.
